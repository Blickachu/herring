Solvers

  • Nutrimatic -- finds words matching more complicated constraints; usually gets overloaded during the Hunt
  • OneLook -- finds words matching constraints that might include part of speech or meaning
  • anagrampage is an interface for interactively completing anagrams.
  • One Across -- crossword clue solver
  • Solvertools
  • Quinapalus -- finds words matching simple constraints
  • Qat -- finds words matching very complicated constraints, including equations between words
